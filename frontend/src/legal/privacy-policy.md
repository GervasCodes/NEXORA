## 1. What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data NEXORA collects from Buyers, Sellers, and
Delivery Agents on the Platform, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

## 2. Information we collect

**You give us directly:**
- Account details: name, email, phone number, password (stored hashed), and — for
  Sellers — store/business information and account-verification documents.
- Order details: delivery address, items purchased, order notes.
- Payment details: NEXORA does not store full card or mobile-money credentials; these
  are handled by our payment providers (mobile money gateways, PayPal, Selcom, Snippe,
  Stripe as applicable). We store transaction references, amounts, and status.
- Content you create: reviews, dispute messages, evidence photos, chat messages between
  Buyers, Sellers, and Delivery Agents.

**We collect automatically:**
- Device and usage data: IP address, browser/app version, pages viewed, and actions
  taken, used for security, fraud detection, and improving the Platform.
- Location data: a Delivery Agent's live location is shared with the Buyer and Seller
  only while a delivery assigned to them is in progress, for tracking purposes.
- Push notification tokens and email delivery status, if you've enabled notifications.

**We do not knowingly collect** more personal data than needed to run the marketplace,
and we do not collect sensitive categories of data (health, biometric, etc.) except
where a Seller's own product category unavoidably requires it — in that case, that
Seller is separately responsible for lawful handling of that data.

## 3. How we use your information

| Purpose | Example |
|---|---|
| Operate the marketplace | Process orders, route deliveries, calculate Seller payouts and NEXORA's commission. |
| Trust and safety | Detect fraud and abuse, review disputes, enforce the Terms of Service. |
| Communication | Order confirmations, delivery status, dispute updates, account notices — via in-app notification, push, and email. |
| Improve the Platform | Understand usage patterns, fix bugs, and build features. |
| Legal compliance | Respond to lawful requests from authorities, tax and accounting obligations. |

## 4. Who we share information with

- **Between transaction parties**: a Buyer's delivery address and contact details are
  shared with the Seller and assigned Delivery Agent for that order only; a Seller's
  store details are visible to Buyers who order from them.
- **Payment processors**: mobile money providers, PayPal, Selcom, Snippe, and Stripe
  process payments on our behalf under their own privacy terms.
- **Service providers**: cloud hosting, file storage (for photos and evidence), email
  delivery, and push-notification providers, bound by confidentiality obligations.
- **Legal and safety**: when required by law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect the
  rights, property, or safety of NEXORA, our users, or the public.
- We do **not** sell personal data to third parties for their own marketing purposes.

## 5. Data retention

We keep account and order data for as long as your account is active and for a further
period afterward to meet accounting, tax, dispute-resolution, and fraud-prevention
obligations. Dispute evidence and messages are retained as part of the permanent audit
trail for the order in question. You can request deletion of data we're not legally
required to keep (see Section 7).

## 6. Data security

We use industry-standard measures — encrypted connections, hashed passwords, and
access controls limiting who inside NEXORA can view personal data — to protect your
information. No system is perfectly secure, and you should also protect your own
account (a strong password, not sharing OTP codes, and reporting suspicious activity
promptly).

## 7. Your rights and choices

Subject to applicable law, you can:
- Access and update most of your profile information directly from Account settings.
- Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- Request deletion of your account and associated personal data, subject to the
  retention needs described in Section 5 (e.g. we can't delete order records needed
  for a currently open dispute or a tax-relevant transaction).
- Opt out of non-essential push and marketing email notifications from Account settings
  (order and dispute-related notifications are essential to the service and can't be
  disabled while you have open orders).

To exercise any of these, use the support contact listed in your Account settings.

## 8. Children

The Platform is not directed at children under the age required to form a binding
contract in your jurisdiction, and we don't knowingly collect personal data from them.

## 9. International transfers

Because Buyers, Sellers, and Delivery Agents may be in different regions, your data may
be processed in a country other than your own. Where required, we rely on the
safeguards required by applicable law for such transfers.

## 10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Platform evolves. Material changes will be flagged in
the Platform with a new effective date above.

## 11. Contact

Questions about this policy, or requests relating to your personal data, can be sent
through the support contact listed in your Account settings.
